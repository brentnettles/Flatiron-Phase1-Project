        About Us

        Greetings from Le Fur Plat! As the owner, it's with great pride that I share the illustrious history of our cherished restaurant. Since its establishment in 1975 in Lyon, France, Le Fur Plat has epitomized the essence of French culinary artistry. Named in honor of my grandfather, Chef Henri Le Fur, our establishment has evolved from a humble bistro to a beacon of gastronomic excellence, earning accolades including prestigious Michelin stars. Our commitment to culinary mastery is unwavering, rooted in the meticulous selection of locally sourced ingredients and the fusion of traditional techniques with innovative flavors. Each dish crafted in our kitchen tells a story of passion and dedication, inviting guests to embark on a journey of sensory delight. With warm hospitality and an ambiance reminiscent of a classic French brasserie, Le Fur Plat continues to captivate discerning palates and cultivate lasting memories. Join us as we celebrate the timeless art of French cuisine and the legacy of Le Fur Plat.We are passionate about serving delicious meals made from the freshest ingredients.
